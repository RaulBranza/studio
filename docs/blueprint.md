# **App Name**: ClipCut Barbershop

## Core Features:

- Homepage Showcase: Display a captivating hero section with high-quality images showcasing the barbershop's atmosphere, services, and satisfied customers.
- Services Overview: Detail all services offered, including haircuts, styling, beard trims, and special treatments, with pricing information.
- Image Gallery: Feature a visual gallery of the barbershop's best work, demonstrating the skills and creativity of the barbers.
- Online Appointment Booking: Integrate a simple booking system for clients to schedule appointments easily.
- Styling Tool: Allow clients to upload their picture, and then using generative AI as a tool, try out different haircuts.
- Contact Information: Provide the barbershop's address, phone number, email, and opening hours, along with a contact form for inquiries.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Rich brown (#A67B5B) to convey a classic, trustworthy, and grounded aesthetic.
- Background color: Very light brown (#F5F0EC), near off-white, which will nicely offset the primary and accent colors in a light color scheme.
- Accent color: Burnt orange (#D98E41) for call-to-action buttons and highlights, lending the site a little more visual pop. 
- Body and headline font: 'Literata' serif, lending an inviting, refined and contemporary feel to the overall design
- Use clean and modern icons related to barbershop services (scissors, comb, razor) to enhance the user interface.
- Design a simple and intuitive layout with clear sections for easy navigation and readability.
- Incorporate subtle animations for a smooth user experience when navigating between sections or viewing content.
